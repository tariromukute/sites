Quality services that exceeds the expectations

Our business offers Graphic Designing, Printing and Sound Equipment hiring for live memorable events such as wedding, corporate events, launches, anniversaries just to mention a few. We transform the unique and personal vision you have for your event to reflect it on your event day.

Graphic Printing

This is an instant way whereby a graphic artwork is send direct from a computer to a digital printer. It is the most quickest way to produce small quantities of products such as business cards, certificates, fliers, posters, calenders, books etc. We also do lithograph printing and heat pressing.

Graphic Design

Our Graphic Designers have vast experience in using all versions of Corel draw and Adobe Photoshop which are the main programs used in producing neat and presentable designs. We also offer graphic designing lessons to those interested at agreed and flexible time tables .

Sound Equipment

Our system is made up of quality brands which gives a crystal clear sound. We supply both Cordless (wireless) and wired microphones for each event. Our system are from well known brands that is Samson, Gemini, Takstar, Shure, Hybrid and Behringer.

Signage

Refers to the production of Vinyl stickers and PVC banners to produce signs like bill boards, shop branding, vehicle branding, in-house information signs etc. We also produce variety of stickers which includes Car magnets, Fridge magnets, Bottle labels, vehicle licence disc holder and vehicle wrap.
